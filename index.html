About The Rounds

Prizes

Along with the fun of solving problems, there are prizes in cash amounting to INR 40000 to be won. Winning team will also receive the Humblefool Trophy.T-Shirts Is Also A Part Of The Exciting Prizes

About

Humblefoolcup is a contest under Aparoksha 2016, annual Tech Fest of IIIT Allahabad conducted in the memory of Harsha SuryaNarayana A.K.A Humblefool. Harsha Suryanarayana, popularly known as humblefool to the coding community, and as Lord among his friends and the people close to him. He Died in an accident on the night of 15th June'14



Event Timeline

  • First Round :Online. March 2017
    The online round will be held world wide and Top 50 participants will get selected to the final round.
  • Cup Final. March 2017
    Will be held during Aparoksha at IIIT-A.Top teams will arrive at Allahabad for the final showdown
